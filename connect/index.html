{Infinite Confusion}
Paul Joseph
This classic '94 model DUDE (limited edition) features a unique blend of quirky charm and geeky enthusiasm. Equipped with green eyes and a certified fondness for long walks on the beach (because, who doesn’t love a good sunset?), he’s notorious for his inability to take life too seriously—unless, of course, we’re talking about gaming.

When he's not busy cracking bad jokes or trying to convince his friends that pineapple on pizza is a valid topping, he’s out on a mission to recycle and refurbish as many retro consoles as possible. Why? Because nothing says ‘hero’ quite like bringing vintage gaming joy back to life.

With a controller in hand and a head full of pixelated dreams, he’s dedicated to ensuring that no console gets left behind. So, join him on this epic quest, and together, let’s rescue those dusty gems from attics and basements everywhere!
